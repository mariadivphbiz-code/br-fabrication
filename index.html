About B&R Fabrication

A father-and-son fabrication company built on quality work.

B&R Fabrication was established in 2002 to provide reliable, quality metal fabrication and millwright work for the industrial and agricultural sectors in Southern California.

The company is the realization of a father and son partnership, Roy and Brian DeFisher, with life-long careers in welding, fabrication and millwright work.
